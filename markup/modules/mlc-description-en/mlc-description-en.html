The capacity of the module depends on the number of the lasers installed and may consist from 15–16 pcs/min (with one laser) up to 70–75 pcs/min (with three lasers working simultaneously with three glasses).

MLC is able to work with and under control of almost any blowing machine. To estimate the final construction of the module and to prepare the appropriate technical task it is needed to get acquainted with the factory and blowing machine of the customer.

* in case of necessity the construction of the module may be adjusted to the requirements of the customer
