ULSP

Coating evaporation laser table (ULSP) removes electric conductive coating from the surface of low-emissivity glass with a speed of 360 mm²/sec. Software supplied together with the table, controls temperature values in each spot of glass of any shape.

This would mean that uniform heating can be provided or, vice versa, a desired difference in temperature values can be set, depending on the functional load of the glass area (e.g.: windshield wipers’ parking area additional heating).
